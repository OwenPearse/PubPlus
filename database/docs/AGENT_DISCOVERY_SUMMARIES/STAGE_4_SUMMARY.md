Stage 4 — Owner & Business Data

Purpose
Define the owner/business side of PubPlus so the owner portal can grow cleanly without corrupting the consumer/public truth model.

Locked outcomes

Business entity / operator group is first-class.
Owner user accounts are separate from businesses and separate from venues.
One business may manage multiple venues.
Multiple owner-side users may exist per business and per venue.
A venue may have multiple concurrent approved managing businesses.
Venue access must come through an explicit approved business-to-venue management relationship.
Claim initiation, verification, active management rights, and venue-scoped permissions are distinct concepts.
Authority must flow through an explicit layered chain, not shortcuts.
Public truth, owner-private operational data, workflow/history, authority workflow history, commercial/subscription data, and user-private data are distinct domains.
Submission records are not truth; workflow history is not live authority; commercial state is not operational truth.

Manager refinements / approvals

Do not allow convenience shortcuts such as:
direct person-to-venue authority
claim-to-access shortcut
verification-to-access shortcut
business-membership-to-access shortcut
portal-login-to-access shortcut
Subscriptions and core commercial entitlements should attach primarily to the business entity, with venue-scoped overlays where needed.

Main guardrails

No generic “owner metadata” blob.
No collapse of business, venue relationship, permissions, and workflow into one loose role system.
Preserve layered authority and explicit domain separation.
